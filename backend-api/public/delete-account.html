How to Request Account Deletion

We respect your privacy and your right to control your data. If you wish to permanently delete your URSArts account and associated data, follow these steps:

1 Open the URSArts App

Launch the URSArts mobile application on your Android device.

2 Go to Your Profile

Tap the Profile icon at the bottom of the screen to access your account settings.

3 Navigate to Settings

Look for Settings or Account Settings option in your profile menu.

4 Select Delete Account

Find and tap the Delete my account or Delete Account option.

5 Confirm Deletion

Read the confirmation message carefully, then confirm that you want to permanently delete your account.

6 Deletion Complete

Your account will be deleted, and you will be logged out of the app.

⚠ Important: Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. Once deleted, you cannot recover your account, order history, or associated data.

What Data Gets Deleted?

When you delete your URSArts account, the following information is permanently removed from our servers:

Data Type Status After Deletion Retention Notes
Account Profile (name, email, password) Deleted Immediately removed
Contact Information (phone, addresses) Deleted Immediately removed
Shopping Cart Deleted Immediately removed
Order History (past orders, transactions) Anonymized Retained for 7 years (legal/tax compliance)
Payment Information (Razorpay refs) Anonymized Retained for 7 years (financial records)
Reviews & Ratings Deleted or anonymized May be kept anonymously for product integrity
Communication Records (support emails) Partially retained May be kept for dispute resolution (6 months)
Note: Some data may be retained in anonymized or aggregated form for legal compliance, fraud prevention, and system security purposes.

Data Deletion Without Account Deletion

We understand that you may want to control your data without fully deleting your account. Currently, URSArts offers the following options:

  • Clear Shopping Cart: You can clear your cart anytime in the app without deleting your account.
  • Remove Addresses: You can delete individual saved addresses from your profile.
  • Request Partial Data Removal: Contact us at srinivasu@gmail.com to request deletion of specific data while keeping your account active (e.g., delete order history but keep account).
Partial Data Deletion: If you want to delete specific information (e.g., personal addresses, order history) but keep your account, please email us with details of what you'd like removed. We will process your request within 30 days.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does account deletion take?

Account deletion is processed immediately when you confirm in the app. However, data may take up to 30 days to be fully removed from our backup systems.

Can I recover my account after deletion?

No. Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. If you change your mind, you will need to create a new account with the same or different email.

What happens to my pending orders after I delete my account?

If you have pending orders, you must complete or cancel them before deleting your account. Contact support at srinivasu@gmail.com if you have active orders.

Will my account be visible to sellers after deletion?

No. Your account and personal information will not be visible to sellers after deletion. However, anonymized order records may be retained for legal purposes.

What if I need to prove I deleted my account?

Send an email to srinivasu@gmail.com with your original email address. We can provide confirmation of account deletion.

Can I delete my account on web or desktop?

Currently, account deletion is only available through the URSArts mobile app. For alternative deletion methods, contact support.

How do I request deletion of specific data categories?

Email srinivasu@gmail.com with a clear list of data you want deleted (e.g., "delete order history but keep account"). We will respond within 30 days.

Legal Information: We comply with data protection laws including but not limited to GDPR, CCPA, and local regulations. Some data may be retained as required by law for tax, legal, and security purposes.
